1. RICH DAD POOR DAD

Writer - Robert T Kiyosaki

"Rich Dad Poor Dad" by Robert Kiyosaki contrasts his two "dads"—his real father and his best friend's father—to illustrate different attitudes toward money and investing. The book advocates financial education, investing in assets, and developing passive income streams to achieve financial independence, challenging conventional beliefs about work and wealth.

2. ATOMIC HABITS

Writer - James Clear

"Atomic Habits" by James Clear is a transformative guide to forming good habits and breaking bad ones. Clear's practical advice and scientific approach make it easy to understand and apply. With engaging anecdotes and actionable steps, this book is a must-read for anyone looking to improve their life and achieve lasting success.



3. THINK AND GROW RICH

Writer - Napolean Hill

"Think and Grow Rich" by Napoleon Hill distills insights from successful individuals into a practical guide for achieving wealth and success. Emphasizing the power of thoughts, desire, and persistence, Hill outlines principles such as goal-setting, visualization, and faith. The book remains influential for its motivational and personal development strategies.

4.  THE ALCHEMIST

Writer - Paulo Colheo

"The Alchemist" by Paulo Coelho follows Santiago, a shepherd who dreams of finding treasure in Egypt. His journey teaches him about the importance of following one's dreams, listening to the heart, and recognizing the interconnectedness of all things. The novel is celebrated for its philosophical depth and inspiring message.
